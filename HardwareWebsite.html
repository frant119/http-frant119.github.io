Hardware
This page will explain 5 essential hardware devices for a computer.
Motherboard Power Supply Harddrive PCI Card DvD Drive
The Motherboard is the main component of the computer. It has multiple different parts connected to it to make it one of the most important hardware of the computer. The Power Supply transports the energy to the computer. It is very important for the computer to store energy. The Harddrive stores all of your data and information of your computer. It is where the computer dumps all of the information and files for later use. The PCI Card is the graphics card for the computer. It runs the graphics and how good the graphics look through your monitor. The DvD Drive allows you to put a dvd in a computer that can run a specific program, when dvd is inserted.
